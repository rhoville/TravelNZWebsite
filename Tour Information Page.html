Auckland  Tour Package

Tour Details

Day 1: Arrival in Auckland

  • Airport pickup and transfer to hotel
  • Free time to explore the city

    • Day 2: Auckland City Tour

  • Visit the iconic Sky Tower
  • Explore Auckland's waterfront and harbor
  • Visit Auckland Museum and learn about Maori culture
  • Afternoon visit to the beautiful Auckland Domain gardens

    Day 3: Waiheke Island Tour

  • Catch a ferry to Waiheke Island, known for its vineyards and beautiful beaches
  • Visit local wineries for tastings and tours
  • Relax on the beach and enjoy the stunning views

    Day 4: Auckland Harbour Cruise

  • Spend the day cruising around Auckland Harbour, taking in the sights of the city skyline, the iconic Harbour Bridge, and surrounding islands.
  • Enjoy a light lunch onboard and have the option to disembark and explore the quaint seaside village of Devonport.

    Day 5: Departure 

  • Airport transfer for departure
